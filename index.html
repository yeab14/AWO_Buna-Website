Discover the Heart of Ethiopia, in Every Sip

AWO Coffee Company is an Ethiopian enterprise focused on cultivating, roasting, and exporting high-quality specialty coffee. With a blend of traditional farming and modern quality control, AWO offers premium Arabica beans to global wholesale clients, ensuring sustainability and exceptional flavor.

AWO’s farms are located in Ethiopia’s renowned coffee regions, including Yirgacheffe, Bonga Kafa, and Bench Maji. These areas produce unique coffees with vibrant floral notes, tropical fruit flavors, and the famous Gesha variety, prized for its elegance and complexity.
